LGBTQ+ and Bullying


82% had problems with bullying about sexual orientation/gender identity

64% felt unsafe at school due to sexual orientation

44% felt unsafe at school due to gender identity

32% did not go to school for at least one day because they felt unsafe

44% experienced physical harassment

22% experienced violence
61% never reports the attacks

31% said that made no efforts to respond (of those that did report the attacks.)

9 out of 10 LGBTQ+ teens have reported being bullied at school within the past year because of their sexual orientation

28% drop out of school due to harassment
